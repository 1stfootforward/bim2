Welcome to BuildBIM

BuildBIM is a design, drafting and BIM solutions provider for New Zealand and Australian construction industries. We provide a quality service that includes design, drafting and construction documentation, BIM coordination, clash detection, and 3D modelling. We offer fast turnaround times at competitive rates. Our team manages both CAD and BIM projects using Autodesk (Autocad and Revit) and Graphisoft (ArchiCAD) platforms.

A New Zealand company with offices in Christchurch and Santiago Chile, we provide local expertise and the benefits of outsourcing, without the headaches. Our clients include homebuilders and construction companies, architects, developers, and product manufacturers.

Design and Drafting service

BuildBIM Drafting offers full construction documentation services for both residential and commercial builds. BIM Coordination and clash detection

BuildBIM provides building information models that are complete digital representations of a building design process. These models allow designers, contractors, developers, manufacturers and engineers to share data and construction information easily and efficiently, locating potential conflicts between different design plans. Models can also be utilized for Scheduling and Estimation purposes too. We offer complete BIM Coordinated Models which will assist in providing precise scheduling and quantities for estimation.

3D Rendering & Modelling

We convert 2D CAD files into 3D models, providing a better view of the building design and more sophisticated detailing accuracy. In addition, we can render high resolution interior and exterior views for presentations, brochures, banners and poster as well as fly thru video of your building. Our team has the skills and experience to transform your 2d drawings, Autocad plans, 3d Revit or Sketchup files into photo realistic visualizations.
